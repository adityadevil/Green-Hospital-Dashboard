🏥 Green Hospitality Dashboard | Power BI
📌 Project Overview

The Green Hospitality Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze hotel and hospitality business performance. It helps users monitor important business metrics such as revenue, occupancy, bookings, customer ratings, cancellations, and property performance.

The dashboard provides a clear and easy-to-understand view of business data, helping management make better decisions using real-time insights.

🎯 Project Objectives
Monitor overall business performance.
Analyze revenue across cities and properties.
Track occupancy and booking trends.
Compare hotel ratings and customer satisfaction.
Identify high-performing and low-performing properties.
Support data-driven business decisions.
